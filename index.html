Privacy policy

BlobGraffiti is a game developed by Rahulky. This app is provided as a free game and is intended for entertainment purposes only.

Information Collection and Use

BlobGraffiti does not collect or store any personally identifiable information. The game may request access to certain device features for gameplay functionality: Microphone: Used only to detect sound input (to pop blobs). Audio data is processed locally on the device and is never recorded, saved, or shared. Camera (optional): If enabled, it is only used to display your real-world background during gameplay. No images or video are recorded, stored, or transmitted. We do not collect, share, or sell any personal data.

Third-Party Services

Security
